Welcome to Stuypedia!

To save your classes, navigate to "Modify Classes", search for a class, and click the "Add" button next to the class you want to save. Saved classes will have a ⭐ next to their name on their class page.

Data collected from students like you will be displayed on each class page. There, you can see what others students thought of the class and different teachers, as well as recommended resources.
